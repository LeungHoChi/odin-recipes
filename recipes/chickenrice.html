Chicken Rice

Description

This is one of the most-touted must eat foods when you come to Singapore

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Fresh Chicken
  • 2 slices ginger
  • Water

Steps

  1. Clean the chicken cavity thoroughly. Rub inside and outside generously with some salt and then rinse thoroughly and drain. Set aside.
  2. Mix together, Shao Xing/ Rice wine (Or Rice vinegar) with 2 tsp light soya sauce.
  3. Rub inside the cavity of the chicken. Stuff the bird with the ginger, garlic, and spring onion.
