Wanton Mee

Description

This is a recipe for Wanton Mee

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Char Siew
  • Wantons

Steps

  1. Wrap minced meat into Wanton skins. Flavor with seasoning
  2. Let’s make the mushroom sauce! First, soak the mushrooms in warm water until they’re soft, then squeeze out any extra water and keep the liquid.
  3. To make the garlic oil, just heat the oil over low-medium heat and fry the garlic until it’s golden and crispy.
